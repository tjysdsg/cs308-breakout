Rules:
- There are many bricks on the screen and your goal is to
destroy them all.
- A ball moves straight around the screen, bouncing off the
top and two sides of the screen.
- When a brick is hit, the ball bounces back and the brick
is destroyed. Some bricks requires two hit to be destroyed,
and some cannot be destroyed.
- You lose one life when the ball touches the bottom of the
screen, if you have 0 life left, you lose the game.
- There is also a horizontally movable paddle that you can
use to bounce the ball upward, keeping it in play.

               Press Space key to start/pause
